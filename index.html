Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our courses and platform

Do I need prior art experience to enroll?

No prior experience is required for our Foundation Course. Our advanced programs are designed for students with some background in digital art or related fields.

What tools will be covered in the courses?

Our courses include instruction on various AI tools including Runway, Pika, Midjourney, and other digital creative technologies.

How long are the courses?

Course duration varies: Foundation Course (6 weeks), Professional Program (12 weeks), and Specialty Workshops (2-4 weeks each).

Is there ongoing support after course completion?

Students receive access to our community platform and periodic update sessions on new tools and techniques.
